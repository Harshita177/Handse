USER GUIDE

How to Use Handse

Handse is a 2-way sign language translator and detector that supports both American Sign Language (ASL) and Indian Sign Language (ISL). Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1: Select Language

Choose between ASL (English) or ISL (Hindi) for translation.

Step 2: Sign Detection

Use your webcam to perform sign language gestures. Handse will detect and translate them in real-time.

Step 3: Text Input

Type or speak text, and Handse will convert it into sign language animations.

Step 4: Practice & Learn

Use the interactive learning modules to improve your sign language skills.
